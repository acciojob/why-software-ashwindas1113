Why I want to become a developer?

Artificial intelligence (AI) is like a super-smart computer system that's everywhere now, from our phones to helping doctors. It's changing the way we do things, making work faster and even helping us in our daily lives. But, as AI becomes a big part of our world, we need to think about how it affects our privacy and fairness. We also wonder about jobs - will there be enough for people as AI takes over some tasks? So, while AI is cool and helpful, we need rules to make sure it's used in a good way. In schools, AI can help us learn better, but we also need to understand it well. It's not just about the good stuff - we must make sure AI is used in a fair and responsible way. We can use AI to solve big problems like climate change and health issues, but we must be careful how we use it. In the end, AI can make life better, but we have to be smart about it and make sure everyone understands how it works.
